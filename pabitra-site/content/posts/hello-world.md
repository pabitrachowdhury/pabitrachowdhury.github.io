## Why I Started This Blog

I've always believed in the power of sharing knowledge. This blog will be a place where I document my learning, share interesting discoveries, and connect with like-minded people.

## What to Expect

Here's what you can expect from this blog:

- Thoughts on technology and development

This site is built with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/), a fast and flexible static site generator. I'm using the beautiful [Archie theme](https://github.com/athul/archie) for its clean, minimal design that focuses on content.
